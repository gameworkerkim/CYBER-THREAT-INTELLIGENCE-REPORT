### KelpDAO LayerZero Bridge Hack вҖ” A Sophisticated Attack on the Single Point of Failure in Off-Chain Verification Infrastructure

**1-of-1 DVN, RPC node poisoning, and systemic risk spreading across DeFi**

On April 18, 2026, TraderTraitor вҖ” a subgroup of the North Korea-linked Lazarus Group вҖ” attacked KelpDAO's LayerZero bridge infrastructure, stealing **116,500 rsETH (approximately USD 292 million)**. Rather than exploiting a smart contract bug, the attackers precisely targeted the 1-of-1 DVN single point of failure and RPC node poisoning in the off-chain verification infrastructure. This is the **largest DeFi hack of 2026** and the incident that made North Korea account for 76% of global crypto hack losses in 2026 with just two attacks.
